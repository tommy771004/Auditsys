## 8. Designer's Notes

*   **Typography:** Strict adherence to `Inter` for general copy, with `JetBrains Mono` or `Fira Code` for technical highlights and CTAs to reinforce our developer-native positioning.
*   **Color Palette:** We operate in a Dark Mode default space. Maintain deep slate/charcoal backgrounds with Electric Cyan (`#00E5FF`) for primary interactions and Amethyst Purple (`#9D4EDD`) as a secondary AI-assist gradient.
*   **Spacing:** Ensure generous negative space for high legibility, avoiding the "cluttered dashboard" anti-pattern. 
*   **Micro-interactions:** Any button or link interacting with our "agents" should feature subtle glow/blur effects or pulse animations indicating active, background computing tasks. 
*   **Responsive:** Ensure TikTok creatives are cut 9:16 natively, with safe zones strictly obeyed so text doesn't overlap UI. All landing pages must be fluidly responsive across touch devices, prioritizing the core intake input box.
